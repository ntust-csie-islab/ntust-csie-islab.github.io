一種文章分類方法及系統

A METHOD AND SYSTEM FOR CLASSIFYING ARTICLE

摘要

    ﻿      本發明揭露了一種文章分類方法及系統，其除了能對文章的類別進行自動的區別以達到文章分類之目的外，亦能根據最初所準備的第一關鍵字組或是文章，以自動地產出一概觀性文獻。需注意的是，上開的概觀性文獻係具有對應於其之內容的一代表主題，而此代表主題係足以清楚地交代各標的文章所屬之領域。據此，透過上 開的概觀性文獻，本發明得以助使用者得於最短的時間中從中了解文章的主題及其技術類別，進而解決了存在已久的問題。

The present invention discloses a method and system for classifying articles.  The present invention can be not only capable of distinguishing the type of the article but also novelty to generate a summary article automatically in accordance with the initial prepared keyword group or articles.  Furthermore, it is worth a mention that the summary article previously described comprises a representative title in according to the content of the initial prepared articles, wherein the representative title is also able to identify the aspect of the articles.  Accordingly, by the said summary article, the present invention is capable of decreasing the time required to understand the spirit and the technical aspect of the articles so as to solve the long lasted problem of the prior art.
