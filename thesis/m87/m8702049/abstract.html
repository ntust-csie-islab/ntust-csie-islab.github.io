能有效變更之模組化階層式文件分類

摘要

由於網頁文件等線上資訊的激增，要由如此大量的資訊中找出使用者需要的部分是相當困難的工作，因而須自動分類的機制將這些文件依人們的知識分門別類，以便讓搜尋工作能更有效率。然而，將文件自動分類到一個階層式的分類架構會遭遇到一些困難，如：建立及維護一個龐大的階層式分類機制是相當費時的；再者，階層式分類會遭遇相似文件難以分類的問題。在本篇論文理我們提出了模組化階層式分類 (modularized hierarchical classification) 搭配兩階段決策 (2-phase decision) 來解決上述問題。應用此方法可以快速建立一個階層式分類機制，並具有不錯的效能。每當分類裡的文章有變動時， 分類機制可以快速的調整以適應新的文件分布。同時，兩階段決策提昇了相似文件的分類的效能。我們的實驗也顯示這樣的分類確實能幫助文件做階層式的分類。

A Modularized Hierarchical Document Classification with Efficient Modification

Abstract

Because of the proliferation of online information, such as WWW documents, mining for interests from a great quantity of information becomes a terrible and difficult task. Therefore, automatic document classification is urgently needed to organize documents toward human knowledge for efficient searching. However, classification with hierarchical document taxonomy suffers from some problems. One is the costly expense of computation while establishing and updating the classification scheme corresponding to a huge document hierarchy. Another is the unbalanced similarity of categories, which makes classifiers even harder to distinguish an appropriate category from the similar categories. In this thesis, we present a method, namely, modularized hierarchical classification with 2-phase decision, to solve the problems. Indeed, we can rapidly establish classification scheme and still have a fairly good accuracy rate of classification in individual level of hierarchical document taxonomy. Whenever documents are added or removed in the document hierarchy, the corresponding classification scheme can be quickly adjusted to suit the renewed hierarchy. Meanwhile, the accuracy rate can be promoted by using 2-phase decision. Experiments suggest that our classification scheme is adequate to aid the hierarchical classification.
