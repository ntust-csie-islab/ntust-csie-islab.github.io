以關聯式規則探勘為基礎建構關聯式索引典用於互動式查詢擴展

摘要

網際網路已成為資訊傳播與溝通的主要媒介，人們都經由瀏覽網站，或者利用搜尋服務來 找尋所想要的資訊。在資訊搜尋的過程中，使用者所輸入的查詢字，可決定資料的搜尋範 圍，但也同時影響了其與欲找尋的資料之間的適切性，所以查詢字與欲查詢資料內容之間 的誤差，儼然已成為重要的課題。針對此問題，我們以關聯式索引典來提供互動式查詢擴 展。為了建構關聯式索引典，我們首先假設某些點選的網頁即使並非該使用者真正所想要 ，但每一筆使用者查詢歷史紀錄中的點選網頁仍皆互有語意關係；接著我們採用關聯式規 則技術，來探勘使用者查詢歷史紀錄中的點選網頁，試圖從中找到字與字的非對稱性相互 關聯來達成建構之目的。而這種互動式擴展查詢功能，僅使用資料庫的結構化查詢語言， 即可從關聯式索引典中找尋候選擴展字供使用者選擇。如果因所蒐集的使用者查詢歷史紀 錄不完整，而造成某些查詢的候選擴展字太少，我們可運用自我組織型映射(SOM)類神經網 路模型中的合作機制概念，來延伸候選擴展字。實驗結果證實，從使用者查詢歷史紀錄中 探勘出字與字的關聯性是可行的；不過，關聯式索引典的品質與完整性，很明顯地受限於 使用者查詢歷史紀錄的蒐集。我們將上述之實驗結果，實際運用在線上學習之課程軟體代 理人的查詢精靈，以增進其搜尋的能力。

Association Thesaurus Construction for Interactive Query Expansion based on Association Rules Mining

Abstract

World Wide Web has become a major medium for information publication and communication.　People either browse or use the search service when they want to find specific information on the web.　The popular way to find desired information is using major search engines.　Vocabulary problem are raised on information retrieval issues.　Our approach to these problems is to provide users with query expansion based on association thesaurus.　To construct association thesaurus, association rule mining is employed to generate asymmetric relationship between terms by relevance feedback.　It is basis of assuming that rated web pages on every search result are correlated even though some rated web pages are not desired for the user.　Hence, rated web pages are transferred to user-interested terms based on terms co-occurrence by ATF-DF measure we propose.　Then these list of user-interested terms are mined to produce association rules.　Interactive query expansion uses structural query language to respond candidate expansion terms for user selecting from association thesaurus.　In addition, novel method whose idea comes from cooperative process of self-organizing map expands candidate expansion terms when rare candidate expansion terms.　Experimental results show that query logs are used to transfer to knowledge is feasible.　The quality and completeness of association thesaurus depend on the query logs collection greatly.　We apply interactive query expansion with association thesaurus to the Query Agent on the Coursebot which is web-based intelligent recommendation system for e-learning.　The Query Agent has improvement of retrieval effectiveness on the Coursebot.　It can increase precision rate of search without poor recall rate.
