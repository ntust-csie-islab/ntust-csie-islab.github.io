Fuzzy Neuron Laboratory Paper

92級畢業碩士 張豪崴 發表論文

摘要

由於網際網路提供了大量且雜亂的資訊，因此使用者需要搜尋引擎來幫助他們找尋需要的資訊。雖然目前一般的搜尋引擎已具備幫助使用者找尋相關資料的能力，但由於網路資料增加得太快，因此遇上了一些問題。這些問題包含搜尋結果的精確度過低以及搜尋系統缺乏重要的領域知識(Domain Knowledge)。處理這些問題的其中一個方法就是建構特定領域搜尋引擎（Topic-Specific Search Engines）。藉由將專家所建立的知識應用於搜尋系統，除了能讓使用者在瀏覽搜尋結果時能更瞭解此領域的知識外，搜尋結果的精確度也會增加。

在此篇論文中，我們將焦點放在服務的搜尋上。我們提出一個名為服務導向式搜尋的系統來幫助使用者搜尋以提供服務資訊為主的網頁。其基本精神在於增加了服務關係（Service Relation）的知識於搜尋系統。服務關係（Service Relation）是由專家所建立的，用來描述服務與服務之間的關係。在我們的系統中，服務關係主要扮演的角色在於建立一個樹狀瀏覽介面與一個新的網頁排序法。此系統主要的貢獻在於能幫助使用者更有組織的找尋他們所欲尋找的資料，且同時提高搜尋結果的準確度。

　

Abstract

    Since vast and heterogeneous documents on the Internet, users need search engines to find their required information. Currently, general-purpose search engines have ability to return some useful search results with respect to users’ queries. But, with rapid growth of the World Wide Web, general-purposed search engines faced with some problems. Three of these problems are limits of making queries, lacking of domain knowledge and low precision of search results. One way to deal with these problems is to build topic-specific search engines. With the domain knowledge provided by experts, making query could be easy for users and the precision of search results can be improvedt

In this study, we focus our topic on “Service”. A new search approach, named as Service-Oriented Search, based on ontology of service relation is proposed. The ontology of service relation constructed by the service developers contains the knowledge of relationship between services. The functions of the ontology are creating a tree-formed category in our portal and ranking the web pages. The tree-formed category is an interactive index that helps users finding relevant web pages. In our ranking method, we calculate distance between verb terms and service terms in order to distill pages that contain information about how to apply for the service. In the experiments, we first show that our system is workable in providing an environment for service experts to construct the frames of service relation. Second, the precision of search results in our system is higher than that in E-government portal.
