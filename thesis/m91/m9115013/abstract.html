TradingDecisionMaker: 以股價序列平滑化及
 趨勢變化推論為基礎之股市交易決策方法

摘要

財經工程其範疇相當廣泛，諸如股價時間序列預測及股市交易之決策。 而近幾年財經預測在學術研究及商業應用的領域中也已逐漸成為一項主要且熱門的課題之一。而股價時間序列亦可視為一群轉折點（Change-points）所形成的序列，如果能夠準確及有效的預測這時間序列將會是交易獲利的重要關鍵。然而，由於寄生於股價序列上的雜訊干擾及可能影響趨勢判讀的股價波動所造成的影響，要準確的預測股價及正確的作出交易決策就成了一項相當困難的問題。

因此在此篇論文中我們提出了一個新的股市交易決策方法，叫做TradingDecisionMaker，這個方法結合了股價序列平滑化及趨勢變化推論兩種想法；藉由股價序列平滑化的概念展現出真正反應了趨勢變化的重要轉折點，並利用趨勢變化推論去辨識這些轉折點的發生。因此，TradingDecisionMaker可以有效的幫助投資者在長期趨勢中得到獲利。同時藉由實驗的分析結果，除了證明TradingDecisionMaker的獲利能力之外，其績效也遠優於買進持有策略的共同基金之績效。

TradingDecisionMaker: Stock Trading Decision by Price Series Smoothing and Tendency Transition Inference

Abstract

Financial engineering, such as stock time series forecasting and stock trading decision, is one of the major topics for researches and commercial applications.  The stock price series is known to have a series of change-points, and good prediction for its movements is the key to successful trading.  However, to make the correct trading decision is a very difficult problem because of the influence of the embedded noise and price fluctuations that confuse the stock trend interpretation.

This thesis proposes a novel stock trading method, named as TradingDecisionMaker, based on price series smoothing to reveal the important change-points which reflect more precisely the changes of stock trend movement, and tendency transition inference to identify these important change-points effectively.  Therefore, TradingDecisionMaker can help investors to get the high profits of the long-term period in the stock market effectively.  The experimental results show that the usefulness of TradingDecisionMaker with respect to profitability capacity and its trading performance outperforms the trading performance of open-end mutual funds and TAIEX using buy-and-hold strategy.
