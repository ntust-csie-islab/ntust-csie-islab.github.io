ESTFastAnnotator: 以蛋白質分類的方式為EST做功能標定

摘要

EST分析是目前的被廣泛使用基因研究方法。EST 是基因的片段，錯誤率也相當的高，但是其所攜帶的coding region具有大量的基因資訊。藉由EST分析來找尋新基因也是目前最有效率的方法。到2002年一月為止，未標定功能的EST已經累積了一千萬筆以上，亟需一個可以快速大量自動分析的EST標定工具來加快基因研究的速度。

ESTFastAnnotator提供一個快速、自動化且系統化的EST功能標定方法，不僅克服EST先天上的限制，更能消弭E value對整個系統的影響。在不同的E value之下，ESTFastAnnotator的精確度及正確率可以維持在91%以上，而執行時間是InterProScan的1/13。ESTFastAnnotator可以尋找更多資訊並正確標定長度較短的EST序列達84.2%以上，而同樣的EST序列InterProScan只能標定34.3%的EST。所標定的功能進一步跟TIGR比較： 66.9%的功能跟TIGR 78.6%的功能是一樣的。由於ESTFastAnnotator主要是對所有能找到的蛋白質序列作共同的整理跟分析，可以找到TIGR無法找到的功能。然而一些特殊不具共通性的特殊功能卻有可能因此被遺漏。

ESTFastNnnotator:EST Function Annotation by Protein Cluster Selection

Abstract

EST analysis has been widely used in gene discovery.  Although the incomplete and high error rate of EST, the coding region of EST still occupies the majority information of genes.  Gene discovery through EST analysis is the most efficient ways.  Large ESTs projects have accumulated over 11.3 million un-annotated files until January 2002, and still increased rapidly.  As of that, a rapid, large-scale, and automatic EST annotation pipeline is essential in genome research. 

ESTFastAnnotator tries to provide a fast, systematic, and automatic EST function annotation method, which overcomes the limitations of EST sequence carried in nature, and eliminates the impact of E value cutting threshold.  The precision and recall rate of ESTFastAnnotator keeps over 91% under different E values, and the total running time is at least 13 times lower than InterProScan (which is mostly composed by sequence-motif search tools).  ESTFastAnnotator can explore more function on short EST sequences than sequence-motif search tools by 84.2% of correct hit rate, as InterProScan is 34.3% on the same EST.  The function annotation by ESTFastAnnotator is further compared with TIGR annotation.  Mostly the function annotated by ESTFastAnnotator and TIGR are the same: 66.9% in ESTFastAnnotator and 78.6% in TIGR.  More function will be found by ESTFastAnnotator for ESTFastAnnotator tries to annotate the EST by an overall consideration of protein search result.  Some specific function will be ignored by ESTFastAnnotator for ESTFastAnnotator make decision and conclusion from the common function of the protein search result.
